FirstGens Platform Development Analysis

Costing Analysis

Tool/Feature Purpose Cost
Circle Hosts community features (mentor directory, live events, discussions) £39–£399/month
Notion Stores mentor and user data £0–£8/user/month
Netlify Hosts website and makes it accessible online £0–£19/month
Custom Backend Replaces Notion with a dedicated database for scalability £50,000–£200,000+ (one-time)
AI Integration Personalises content for users £500–£5,000/month
Cloud Hosting Handles increased traffic and data £200–£2,000/month
Development Team Ongoing updates, bug fixes, and new features £10,000–£50,000/month

Pros and Cons

Approach Pros Cons
Starting with Circle, Notion, and Netlify for MVP Rapid deployment, user-friendly, cost-effective Limited customisation and scalability
Transitioning to a custom solution as the platform grows Full control over features and scalability, protection of intellectual property Higher upfront and ongoing costs, requires technical expertise
Integrating third-party AI services Personalised user experience, faster implementation Ongoing costs, dependence on external providers
Building a custom AI solution Tailored to specific needs, full control over the technology Significant development time and resources required
